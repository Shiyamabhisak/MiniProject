ABOUT JOB RECRUITING PORTAL

A job portal,is an online job board that helps applicants find jobs and aids employers in their quest to locate ideal candidates. Job portals provide a centralized location for employers to post information about job openings.The following are some features about this portal

  • Employers can browse through job seekers' resumes to find potential matches for job openings.
  • Employers can utilize job portal matching technology, allowing the system to find potential matches for employment openings.
  • Job portals offer worldwide access for job seekers to view advertisements, providing employers with a wider variety of applicants and a broader candidate pool.
